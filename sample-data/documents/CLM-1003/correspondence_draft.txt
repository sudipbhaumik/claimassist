Dear Gregory Hall,

We are writing in reference to your insurance claim **CLM-1003**, filed under policy **POL-5503**, concerning reported water damage in the basement of the insured property.

Following a full review of the claim details, supporting materials, and applicable policy language, we have completed our coverage assessment. We regret to advise that the claim is **denied**.

Our investigation determined that the damage resulted from water intrusion caused by a backed-up and flooded drain, leading to water entering the basement area. Under your policy terms, this type of loss is specifically excluded.

In accordance with the **Water Damage and Flood Exclusion** provision in **POL-5503**, coverage is not provided for loss caused by flood, surface water, or water backing up through sewers, drains, or similar systems. As the proximate cause of the damage falls within this exclusion, the claim is not eligible for coverage.

Therefore, no payment or indemnity will be issued for this loss.

If you have any questions regarding this determination or would like clarification of the policy wording, please contact our office and reference the claim number listed above.
