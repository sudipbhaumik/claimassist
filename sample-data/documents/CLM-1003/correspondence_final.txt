Dear Gregory Hall,

We are writing regarding your claim **CLM-1003**, submitted under policy **POL-5503**, relating to reported water damage to the basement of the insured property.

After a thorough review of the reported loss, supporting documentation, and the applicable policy provisions, we have completed our coverage determination. We regret to inform you that the claim is being **denied**.

The investigation confirms that the loss resulted from water intrusion originating from a flooded drain and associated backup of water into the basement area. Under the terms and conditions of your policy, specifically the **Water Damage and Flood Exclusion**, coverage does not apply to losses caused by flood, surface water, storm surge, or water backing up through sewers, drains, or similar systems.

As stated in **POL-5503 – Exclusions Section**, any loss where rising or backed-up water from outside or through drainage systems is the proximate cause of damage is excluded from coverage. Based on this provision, the reported damage is not eligible for payment under your policy.

Accordingly, no benefits or indemnity are payable for this claim.

We understand that this determination may be disappointing. If you have any questions regarding this decision or would like to discuss the policy provisions in further detail, please contact our office and reference the claim number above.

Sincerely,
