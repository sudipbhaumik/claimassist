## DAMAGE NARRATIVE

The insured reported water intrusion into the basement area following an overflow
event originating from a flooded floor drain on the DOL. Inspection confirmed
standing water damage affecting flooring materials, lower wall sections, baseboards,
and stored personal property. Moisture readings indicate elevated saturation in
drywall and subfloor materials requiring removal and drying procedures.
