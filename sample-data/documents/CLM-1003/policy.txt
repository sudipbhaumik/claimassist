# COVERAGE

## Section I – Dwelling Coverage

The insurer provides coverage for direct physical loss to the dwelling structure on the insured premises caused by covered perils including fire, lightning, windstorm, hail, explosion, and accidental discharge or overflow of water from plumbing, heating, or air conditioning systems.

## Section II – Other Structures

Coverage applies to structures on the insured premises that are set apart from the dwelling, including garages, sheds, and fences, when damage results from covered perils.

## Section III – Personal Property

The insurer will pay for direct physical loss to personal property owned or used by the insured while located on the insured premises, subject to policy limits and exclusions.

Covered perils include:

* Fire or smoke
* Windstorm or hail
* Theft
* Vandalism
* Falling objects
* Sudden and accidental water discharge from internal plumbing systems

## Section IV – Loss of Use

If a covered loss makes the insured premises uninhabitable, the insurer will pay additional living expenses incurred by the insured, including temporary housing and increased cost of living, subject to applicable limits.

---

# EXCLUSIONS

This policy does not insure against loss caused directly or indirectly by:

1. Intentional acts, neglect, or criminal conduct by the insured.
2. Wear and tear, deterioration, mold, rot, corrosion, or pest infestation.
3. Earth movement, including earthquake, landslide, sinkhole, or mine subsidence.
4. War, nuclear hazard, or governmental action.
5. Mechanical breakdown or inherent defect.
6. Business-related inventory or commercial operations unless endorsed.
7. Pollution or contamination unless caused by a covered peril.
8. **Water damage or flood-related loss, including but not limited to: flood, surface water, storm surge, groundwater seepage, or water backing up through sewers, drains, or sump pumps. Any loss where rising water from outside the insured premises is the proximate cause of damage is excluded from coverage.**

# CONDITIONS

1. **Notice of Loss**
   The insured must provide prompt notice of any loss, including date of loss (DOL), location, and description of damages.

2. **Protection of Property**
   The insured must take reasonable steps to protect property from further damage following a covered loss.

3. **Proof of Loss**
   The insured must submit a sworn proof of loss upon request and provide supporting documentation reasonably required for claim evaluation.

4. **Inspection and Investigation**
   The insurer reserves the right to inspect damaged property and investigate the cause of loss prior to settlement.

5. **Loss Settlement**
   Covered losses will be adjusted based on replacement cost or actual cash value, as applicable under policy terms, less depreciation and deductible.

6. **Subrogation**
   The insurer may recover amounts paid from responsible third parties to the extent of payment made under this policy.

7. **Fraud or Misrepresentation**
   This policy is void in any claim involving concealment, misrepresentation, or fraud by the insured.

8. **Cancellation and Non-Renewal**
   This policy may be cancelled or non-renewed in accordance with Texas insurance law and policy provisions.

9. **Territory**
   Coverage applies only to the insured premises described herein unless otherwise endorsed.

10. **Entire Contract**
    This policy, including declarations, endorsements, and attached forms, constitutes the entire contract of insurance.
