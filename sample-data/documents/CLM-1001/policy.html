This policy provides insurance subject to the terms, conditions, exclusions, limits of liability, and deductibles stated herein. Coverage applies only to covered losses occurring during the policy period and within the applicable policy territory.

COVERAGE

Part A – Liability Coverage

The insurer will pay damages for bodily injury and property damage for which the insured becomes legally liable because of an auto accident arising out of the ownership, maintenance, or use of a covered auto. The insurer also has the duty to defend the insured against covered claims or suits until the applicable limit of liability has been exhausted.

Part B – Medical Payments

Reasonable and necessary medical and funeral expenses incurred by the insured or eligible occupants of a covered auto as the result of an accident are covered, regardless of fault, provided such expenses are incurred within the time period specified by this policy following the DOL.

Part C – Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist Coverage

The insurer will pay compensatory damages that the insured is legally entitled to recover from the owner or operator of an uninsured or underinsured motor vehicle because of bodily injury sustained in a covered accident.

Part D – Physical Damage Coverage

Collision

Covers direct and accidental loss to a covered auto caused by collision with another vehicle or object or by overturn.

Other Than Collision (Comprehensive)

Covers direct and accidental loss caused by:

  • Theft or attempted theft
  • Fire or explosion
  • Vandalism or malicious mischief
  • Windstorm or hail
  • Falling objects
  • Glass breakage
  • Riot or civil disturbance
  • Contact with birds or animals

Payment for covered physical damage is limited to the lesser of the actual cash value, repair cost, or replacement cost, less the applicable deductible.

EXCLUSIONS

  1. Intentional loss or damage caused by or at the direction of the insured.
  2. Loss arising from criminal acts or fraudulent conduct.
  3. Damage resulting from normal wear and tear, deterioration, corrosion, rust, freezing, mechanical or electrical breakdown, or manufacturer defects.
  4. Loss occurring while a covered auto is used in organized racing, speed contests, or competitive driving events.
  5. Damage caused by war, insurrection, nuclear hazard, or governmental confiscation.
  6. Loss resulting from unauthorized commercial use unless specifically endorsed.
  7. Damage caused by pollutants or hazardous contaminants except where expressly covered.
  8. Water damage, flooding, surface water, tidal water, overflow of any body of water, storm surge, mudflow, or water entering from rising floodwaters. For CLM-1003 property claims, any loss where flood or water intrusion is the proximate cause of damage is excluded and no coverage is afforded under this policy.

CONDITIONS

  1. Notice of Loss. The insured must promptly notify the insurer of any accident or loss, including the DOL, location, description of the event, and supporting documentation.
  2. Duties After Loss. The insured shall protect the covered auto from further damage, cooperate during the investigation, and permit inspection of damaged property.
  3. Cooperation. The insured must cooperate in the investigation, settlement, or defense of any claim.
  4. Proof of Loss. Upon request, the insured shall submit a signed proof of loss and provide documentation reasonably necessary to evaluate the claim.
  5. Settlement Options. The insurer may repair, replace, or pay the actual cash value of damaged property subject to applicable limits and deductibles.
  6. Subrogation. Upon payment of a covered loss, the insurer acquires the insured's recovery rights against responsible third parties.
  7. Fraud or Misrepresentation. This policy is void with respect to any claim involving intentional concealment, material misrepresentation, or fraud by the insured.
  8. Cancellation and Non-Renewal. This policy may be cancelled or non-renewed in accordance with applicable law and policy provisions.
  9. Territory. Coverage applies only within the policy territory unless modified by endorsement.
  10. Policy Changes. No change to this policy shall be valid unless made by written endorsement issued by the insurer.

This policy is a legal contract between the insurer and the insured. Coverage is subject to all terms, exclusions, conditions, deductibles, endorsements, and limits stated herein.
