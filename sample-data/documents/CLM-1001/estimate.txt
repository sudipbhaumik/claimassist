## Damage Narrative

Following inspection of the covered vehicle, damage consistent with a rear-end collision was observed. The insured reported that the vehicle sustained impact to the rear while stopped in traffic on the DOL. Visible damage includes deformation of the rear bumper assembly, damage to the trunk lid, and associated cosmetic damage to adjacent panels. No structural frame damage was identified during this preliminary assessment; however, additional concealed damage may be identified during the repair process.
